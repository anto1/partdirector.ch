What does Dürer do?

We publish cards that our community of authors makes, give users the opportunity to apply them in their projects, and artists earn money by creating author's modifications of artificial intelligence

Why are we making Dürer?

We believe that AI technology is a tool that can help everyone realize their ideas and make their daily life more convenient. Now only people with professional knowledge can make their cards

We want all people who have imagination and imagination to be able to train artificial intelligence and help each other realize their dreams

What is a card?

The card is a modification of the artificial intelligence model, which allows you to implement the author's ideas
