WORK

Currently in Yandex Fintech,
head of visual communications

I have been working at Yandex since 2016. This is one of the largest IT companies in Russia, which was traded on Nasdaq before the war.

The company's business is based on search advertising, but it also has many other verticals. I am head of visual communications in the Fintech Department, and before that I was responsible for the communication design of Yandex Classifieds.

In Fintech department I organized an inhouse design studio. The studio creates the entire communication design for Yandex Bank and the Split BNPL service. These are tasks for branding design, performance design materials, banners, in-up communication, web design, landing pages, merch, photography and special projects.

2016—2021, Yandex Classifieds,
head of visual communications

Yandex Classifieds include 4 verticals: auto, real estate, horizontal classifieds and employment. In 2015, Yandex acquired the most popular car classifieds site in Russia — Auto.ru. I rebranded and created an inhouse design studio to solve marketing tasks. In 5 years we have grown this business from 1 to 10. We have grown the market share from 40% to 70%. We have also launched an offline dealer and service centers, online media about cars and a YouTube channel.

2014—2016, Infographics Studio, team lead

In the studio we created interactive infographics on cultural and historical topics. I was a team leader in a team of designer, illustrator and editor.
