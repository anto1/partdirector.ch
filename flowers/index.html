Olya Virich, photographer, 31, born and raised in Krasnodar (Russian south).

There was no photography department in the city, so she graduated from Krasnodar State Institute of Culture with a degree in graphic design.

She started taking photos in 2004. She has been working as a commercial photographer since 2012.

Participant of solo and group exhibitions in Russia, Czech Republic, Finland and France.

Olya is the winner of Grand Prize winner of 5th Annual Photo Annual Awards. Her works were published in the "Out of the phone" photobook as well as in international ad campaigns. She has participated in the international portfolio-review in Moscow.

In an attempt to find her own reflection, Olya scrutinizes everyday life. Her main tools are light, color and poetry.
